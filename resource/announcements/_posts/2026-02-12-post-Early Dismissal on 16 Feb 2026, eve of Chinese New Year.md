<p>Dear Parent/Guardian,</p>
<p>Our school will organizing a series of learning activities as part of
Chinese New Year celebrations on 16 Feb 2026. Students are allowed to wear
Chinese traditional costumes. Those who are not wearing traditional costumes
may come in their orange PE T-shirt with school skirts/ shorts/ pants and
covered school shoes.</p>
<p>The activities will commence from 7.30am to 10.30am, culminating in a
concert at the school hall. Students will be dismissed at about 10.30am
after the concert.</p>
<p>Please be informed that the General Office will be closed at 11.30am on
that day and will remain closed during Chinese New Year on 17 and 18 Feb
2026.</p>
<p>Lessons will resume on 19 Feb 2026 as per normal school timing.</p>
<p>We wish our parents / guardians who are celebrating the Chinese New Year,
a Happy and Prosperous Lunar New Year of the Horse.</p>
<p>Yuan Ching Secondary School</p>
