<h1><strong>Alumna Tng Yi Xin, Cherelle - NP Undergraduate awarded with Diploma with Merit in Chinese Media &amp; Communication Minor in Foreign Languages (Korean)</strong></h1>

<p>Tng Yi Xin, Cherelle, an alumna of Yuan Ching Secondary School, was a
dedicated member of the Chinese Dance team. She demonstrated a strong passion
for language and communication throughout her years at Yuan Ching. Her
time at the school laid a solid foundation for her pursuit of excellence,
nurturing in her the curiosity and dedication that would carry her far
beyond its halls.</p>
<p>Her journey continued at Ngee Ann Polytechnic, where she pursued a Diploma
in Chinese Media &amp; Communication, complementing her studies with a
Minor in Foreign Languages (Korean). Her commitment to her craft and her
relentless pursuit of excellence earned her a Diploma with Merit, as well
as the prestigious August Pictures Silver Medal &amp; Prize — a recognition
of her outstanding contributions and achievements in her field.</p>
<p>Congratulations, Cherelle! We are incredibly proud of you. Your passion
for language, media, and cross-cultural communication is a true reflection
of the values instilled during your time at Yuan Ching. May you continue
to grow, inspire, and make your mark in all that you do. Your dedication
and achievements embody Yuan Ching's vision of "A Thinker, An Achiever,
and a Concerned Citizen".</p>
