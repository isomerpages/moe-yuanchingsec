<h3>Introduction</h3>
<p>The Art &amp; Crafts CCA allows students with a shared interest to come
together and create art as well as sharing knowledge and skills. Through
the weekly sessions, the students explore different art media to produce
art and work together to unleash their creative juices. These activities
provide platforms for students to enhance their creativity, self-expression,
and to gain a greater understanding of art making in a collaborative environment.
Through a myriad of group projects, our CCA members also hone important
life-skills and character that enables them to thrive in the future.</p>
<h3>CCA Activities</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Exploring different art media e.g. acrylic paint, oil paint, printmaking,
sculpture, stop motion animation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in external competitions and exhibitions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in SYF showcase</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in community events</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Achievements</h3>

Shihui, 4E1)</p>
</li>
</ul>
