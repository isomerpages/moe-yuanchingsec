<h3>Introduction</h3>
<p>Yuan Ching Guzheng Ensemble was established in 2005, with the aim of nurturing
students' appreciation for the timeless beauty of the guzheng. Through
their participation in the ensemble, students not only immerse themselves
in classical Chinese music but also explore how this traditional instrument
resonates within modern musical landscapes.</p>
<p>Understanding that musical talent varies, the Yuan Ching Guzheng Ensemble
is committed to providing a supportive environment where students can develop
guzheng skills through dedication and perseverance. Our ensemble encourages
members to embrace challenges, knowing that resilience and hard work are
the keys to mastery. By the end of their journey, students not only acquire
a valuable skill but also discover a meaningful, lifelong passion that
brings joy and fulfilment.</p>
