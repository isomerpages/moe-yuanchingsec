<p><em>The tree’s broad trunk and deep roots symbolise the strong foundation of the PSG. The branches and leaves on the tree show how vibrant and fun the PSG activities are. At the heart of the tree, the PSG is a loving, compassionate and caring community. As the leaves and branches encompass the heart, the parent, the child and the educator are working in harmony. The PSG tree is growing, in its evergreen pasture of Yuan Ching Secondary School.</em>
</p>
<h3>VISION:</h3>
<p>All parents equipped with support and skills working collaboratively to
help the school progress.</p>
<h3>MISSION:</h3>
<p>Strengthening Family Bonds, Deepening Understanding, Enriching Partnerships</p>
<h3>GOALS:</h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Provide opportunities for families to bond together</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide training for parents for better parenting competencies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide opportunities for networking between parent-school and parents-parents</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide platforms for parents to contribute to the school</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h3>KEY PROGRAMMES:</h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Project PAIR (PArents Involved in Reading)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Makan Jalan Excursion</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Night Study Programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Involvement in school activities (eg. Sports Day, Teachers’ Day, Cross-Country)</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The participation of the PSG resulted in providing moments where families
can create memories and bond.</p>
